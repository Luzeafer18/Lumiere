OUR STORY

Nestled in the heart of the city, Lumiere Homestay stands as a rare sanctuary—a place where the warmth of kampung nostalgia is not just preserved but cherished. More than just a homestay, this haven is designed with the belief that "My house is my heaven", a guiding philosophy that shapes every corner of its existence. Run by a local family with deep-rooted values, Lumiere Homestay isn’t just about providing a stay—it’s about creating a home. Every detail, from the rustic wooden interiors to the cozy corners filled with handpicked decor, reflects a commitment to comfort, connection, and heartfelt hospitality. Guests are welcomed like old friends, invited to slow down, unwind, and embrace the familiar essence of kampung life. The village yard is the heart of this retreat—an open space where laughter echoes, stories are shared, and quiet moments feel like a gentle embrace from home. Carefully designed with lush greenery, traditional seating, and a warm communal atmosphere, it offers a rare chance to experience the simplicity and charm of village living. Whether you long for the peaceful rhythms of kampung life or simply seek an escape from the city’s relentless pace, Lumiere Homestay is more than a stay—it’s a journey back to home, back to warmth, back to the roots that remind us what true comfort feels like.
